Yoga

Yoga is much more than just a physical workout — it is a holistic discipline that unites the body, mind, and soul. Originating over 5,000 years ago in ancient India, yoga is both a science and a philosophy designed to bring harmony between human consciousness and universal consciousness.

The word “yoga” comes from the Sanskrit root "yuj", which means to join or to unite — symbolizing the union of the individual self with the higher self or the divine.
